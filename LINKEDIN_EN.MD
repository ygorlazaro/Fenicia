I'm excited to introduce **Fenicia**, an open source, modular, and multi-tenant SaaS platform designed to accelerate B2B and B2C solution development!

## What is Fenicia?

Fenicia is a robust foundation for building scalable SaaS systems, featuring a modern microservices architecture, centralized authentication, physical data isolation per company (multi-tenant), and a Next.js frontend ready for customization.

### Key Features:
- **Physical multi-tenancy**: Each company has its own database.
- **Decoupled microservices**: Auth, Basic, Social, HR, and more.
- **Centralized JWT authentication**: Security and access control across all modules.
- **Next.js frontend**: Modern UI, easy to adapt.
- **RabbitMQ communication**: Internal events and module integration.
- **Ready to scale**: Rate limiting, cache, structured logs, health checks, CI/CD, and more.
